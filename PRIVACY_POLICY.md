# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 03 May 2026_

## Introduction
Revelation is an application for studying the Book of Revelation. The application does not require registration, account login, or payment.

## Data Controller
The data controller is the developer of the Revelation application (hereinafter referred to as “we”).

## Data We Collect
Revelation uses the service Sentry for diagnostics, crash reporting, error tracking, release health monitoring, and aggregated technical usage analytics.

Sentry reports may include technical information such as:
- application version and build number;
- platform and operating system;
- device or runtime diagnostic data;
- selected application language or locale;
- versions of the local database schema and data;
- route or screen name;
- stack traces;
- exception details;
- a technical event trail associated with an error or application session.

We do not use this data to identify individual users.

## Purpose of Data Processing
The collected data is used solely for:
- ensuring the stable operation of the application;
- identifying, analyzing, and fixing errors;
- monitoring the technical health of releases;
- analyzing application usage in an aggregated manner.

## Legal Basis for Processing
Data processing is carried out on the basis of our legitimate interests in accordance with Article 6(1)(f) of the GDPR, namely:
- ensuring the stability, security, and reliability of the application;
- improving the quality of the application.

## Data We Do Not Collect
Revelation does not intentionally collect the following data:
- name;
- email address;
- account identifiers;
- precise location;
- country of residence;
- payment information;
- advertising identifiers.

Sentry is configured in such a way that:
- default personal data collection is disabled;
- screenshots are disabled;
- UI hierarchy capture is disabled;
- textual records of user interactions are disabled.

## Cookies and Tracking
Revelation does not use cookies and does not perform advertising tracking.

We do not sell or share user data with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Data Sharing with Third Parties
Diagnostic data may be transferred to and processed by Sentry as a third-party service provider solely for the purposes described in this policy.

Sentry may process data on servers located outside the European Economic Area (EEA). In such cases, appropriate safeguards are applied, including Standard Contractual Clauses.

For more details, please refer to Sentry’s Privacy Policy:
https://sentry.io/privacy/

## Data Retention
Diagnostic data is retained in Sentry only for the period necessary to analyze and resolve issues, after which it is deleted in accordance with Sentry’s retention policies and settings.

## Security
Diagnostic data is transmitted via secure connections.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

## User Rights
Under the GDPR, you have the right to:
- request access to your data;
- request correction of your data;
- request deletion of your data;
- restrict the processing of your data;
- object to data processing.

Since Revelation does not require accounts and does not collect identifiable data, fulfilling certain requests may be technically limited.

## Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated if the functionality of the application or data processing practices change.

The current version of the policy is always available within the application and on the project page.
